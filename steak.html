Description

Ingredients

  • A5 Waygu steak
  • Pan
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Rosemary and garlic

Steps

  1. Sear steak until crispy
  2. Cook steak until medium rare
  3. Throw butter into pan with garlic and rosemary and baste
  4. Let rest with herbs on top and enjoy!
